Executive Summary

The Strategy: Prioritize conversion-focused Facebook Lead Ads + aggressive short-form video (TikTok / Reels / Shorts) to create a low-friction lead magnet funnel.

The Conversion Engine: Convert leads with a fast follow-up system (SMS + email + remarketing) and high-credibility proof (graduate outcomes, testimonials, micro-projects). This produces the fastest lift in volume + cost-per-enrollment.

Top Priorities (Ranked by Impact)

1. Fix Lead Capture (Immediate)

Switch to Meta Lead Ads + instant server-side sync to CRM.

The Key: Immediate SMS + email auto-response inside 5 minutes. Most conversions are lost here.

2. Short-Form Video (3-4 Weeks)

Produce 8â€“12 TikTok/Reels/Shorts showing outcomes, day-in-the-life, and demo lessons.

Why: Creates huge organic reach and builds trust before the click.

3. High-Intent Remarketing

Build 3 audiences: Viewed LP, Started Application, Watched Webinar.

Run sequential creatives to close the loop.

4. LP Optimization (1 Week)

Implement a single, high-clarity landing page with immediate FAQ, curriculum highlights, and video social proof.

Facebook Ads Strategy

TOF: Awareness

Goal: Video Views / Traffic

Creative: 15-30s Short Videos (Outcomes, Micro-lessons).

Target: Interest (CompTIA, Career Change) & Broad.

MOF: Lead Gen
Primary Driver

Goal: Leads (Instant Forms)

Offer: Free "Intro to Cybersecurity" Live Workshop.

Format: Instant forms for low friction.

BOF: Conversion

Goal: Applications / Enroll

Audience: Webinar attendees, Form openers.

Offer: Limited Seats + Early Bird Discount.

Budget Split (First 60 Days)

  • Test Phase (Day 1-14): 60% TOF (Views), 40% MOF (Lead Ads).
  • Scale Phase (Day 15+): Move budget to best TOF creative & Lookalikes. Increase MOF spend.
